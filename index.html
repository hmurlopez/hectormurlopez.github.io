About Me

I'm Hector, a writer and builder obsessed with one question: how do ordinary people create extraordinary lives?

I spent years searching for shortcuts, only to find there are none. What there is, instead, is a method: build the right skills, acquire the right knowledge, and protect your health. Do that consistently, and compounding does the rest.

Today I write, build, and share frameworks for deliberate growth. My goal is to close the gap between where you are and where you're capable of being, one honest idea at a time.

I publish a weekly newsletter on Substack where I share the frameworks, ideas, and reflections that have shaped how I think and live. No noise, no hacks. Just the work.
